Top Ten UK Visa Refusal Reasons

When you ask others to guarantee that a UK visa will be accepted. The simple answer is: you can't get a 100% guarantee because the Home Office makes the final decision. However, what we can do is make sure your application is complete and submitted correctly to give you the best chance of acceptance.

  • 1. Wrong Documents Supplied. You must provide all the documents needed for your application. Different visa requests mean different sets of requirements. If the wrong documents are submitted, you must submit a new application.
  • 2. Documents Sent in the Wrong Format. The correct UK visa application documents must be sent in the right format. It must be a scanned copy of the original document. If the document is in a language other than English or Welsh, it must be translated.
  • 3. Missing Evidence. Visa application agents can help. To avoid this, the Home Office wants to ensure that all the evidence needed is submitted.
  • 4. Following Guidance from Non-UK Advisers. People make mistakes when making a UK visa refusal case. This is why you should get legal advice from a UK immigration lawyer.
  • 5. Following Advice from Family or Friends. Your family or friends may offer advice that worked for them, but it may not work for you. They cannot advise applicants as individuals.
